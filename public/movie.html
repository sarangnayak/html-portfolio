The Best Movies According to Sarang

My top 5 movies of all-time.

JOHN WICK

This movie series is about action and gun fights, with a role played by Keanu Reeves.

FAST & FURIOUS

This series is about action, drama and cars. The main actor is Vin Diesel as Dom Toretto.

THE TRANSFORMERS

This movie has 5 parts and is a Sci-Fi movie based on advanced robots.

THE AVENGERS

This movie series is also a Sci-Fi series, with 4 parts in the main saga.

IRON MAN

This series has 3 parts and the main character is Tony Stark, played by Robert Downey Jr.
